----------------------About Rajasthan------------------------


We love traditions..

Rajasthan or the Land of Kings is the largest state (area-wise) in India. The state is mainly comprised of the wide and inhospitable Thar Desert. Jaipur is the capital of this beautiful state. The state is gifted with a vast variety of heritage and culture which has been a part of this beautiful land since the times of the most ancient rulers.
